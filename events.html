hydrofarm events

We conduct quarterly events to bring awareness and train hydroponics enthusiasts.

Farm group tours

We organize and accept farm tour reservations for local NGOs, office peers, family and anyone who want to visit our sites so you can see first-hand how we set up and maintain the different types of hydroponics system in our farm.

We also invite resource speakers - experts in the field and hydroponics enthusiasts like you - to give inisghtful talks about their experience in hydroponics and to share their best practices in getting healthy yield of produce. There's also time for questions, so please be sure to come prepared with questions for the speaker, too.

Our on-site experts are always rady to answer your questions regarding our work and the issues surrounding farms and nature
